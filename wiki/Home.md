# InkLink Wiki

InkLink is a companion app for [CrossPoint](https://github.com/xteink/crosspoint-reader) e-reader devices. It lets you manage your books, fonts, files, and wallpapers from your phone over WiFi.

> **Note:** InkLink is under active development. Pages in this wiki are updated as features ship. Sections marked _not yet available_ describe planned functionality.

---

## Getting Started

- [Getting Started](Getting-Started.md): install InkLink and connect your first device

## Using InkLink

- [Sending Books](Sending-Books.md): upload EPUBs and other files to your reader
- [Managing Files](Managing-Files.md): browse, rename, move, and delete files on your device
- [Fonts](Fonts.md): upload `.inx` font packs to your reader
- [Wallpapers](Wallpapers.md): design and push wallpapers optimized for e-ink
- [Device Info](Device-Info.md): view device name, firmware version, and storage usage
- [Multiple Devices](Multiple-Devices.md): manage more than one CrossPoint device
