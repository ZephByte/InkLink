# Sending Books

> _This feature is planned. This page will be updated when book upload ships._

---

## Overview

InkLink lets you send EPUB files from your phone directly to your CrossPoint reader over WiFi. No USB cable or web browser needed. Pick the file and it shows up on your device.

## How to Send a Book

_Steps will be documented once the feature ships._

The planned flow:
1. Tap the **+** or **Send Book** button in InkLink
2. Pick an EPUB from your phone's file browser or Files app
3. Select the target device (if you have more than one)
4. InkLink uploads the file to your reader
5. The book appears on your reader's library on the next refresh

---

## Where Books Are Stored

Books are uploaded to the reader's books directory. The exact path depends on your CrossPoint firmware version.

_To be documented once confirmed against the firmware._

---

## Tips

- Your reader must be in **Sync mode** during upload. See [Getting Started](Getting-Started.md).
- Large files take longer. Keep your reader awake until the transfer completes.
- If the transfer fails, see [Troubleshooting](Troubleshooting.md).
